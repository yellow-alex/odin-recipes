Burger recipe

A burger typically consists of a grilled patty made from ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun. It can be topped with various ingredients like cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments such as ketchup and mustard, making it a popular and versatile meal option.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 teaspoons hot pepper sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¾ cup rolled oats

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat and lightly oil grate
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion soup mix, egg, hot sauce and oats
  3. Shape into 6 patties.
  4. Grill patties over medium high heat for 10 to 20 minutes, or to desired doneness.
